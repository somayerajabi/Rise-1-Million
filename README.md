
This project was completed as part of a Riipen industry capstone collaboration. The objective was to analyze a real-world dataset and generate actionable insights
to support business decision-making.The project focuses on the Leadership survey developed by Rise 1 Million to measure leadership energy sustainability and burnout
visibility across organizations. My primary contributions to this project were running the model and interpreting the results, including data cleaning, exploratory 
analysis, and regression modeling to test relationships between variables related to human energy and burnout.
Several analytical tools and technologies will be used in this project. Python is the primary tool for data analysis, including data cleaning, exploratory analysis, 
statistical testing, and regression modeling to test relationships between variables related to human energy and burnout. Additionally, we will use Power BI to 
create interactive dashboards and visualizations that clearly present patterns, trends, and key insights derived from the analysis.
The project's first objective was to analyze the energy source of performance, confidence in leadership endurance, the burnout measurement system, and the fatigue
impact on decisions. I did some descriptive and exploratory analysis and ran a regression model to figure out the relationship between the years of experience and 
these variables.
Due to confidentiality requirements, the dataset has been anonymized and modified while preserving its analytical structure.
